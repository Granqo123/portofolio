Naruto

It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki,
a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers
and dreams of becoming the Hokage,

The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy,

HunterXHunter
HunterXHunter is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi.
The story focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss
who discovers that his father, who left him at a young age,
is actually a world renowned Hunter,

Code Geass
Code Geass, is a Japanese anime series produced by Sunrise.
Set in an alternate timeline, the series follows the exiled prince Lelouch vi Britannia,
who obtains the "power of absolute obedience"
he leads a rebellion against the rule of the Holy Britannian Empire,
commanding a series of mecha battles.
