Is it only supposed to buy books? Does the store provide with a reading area for all the Otakus wanting to comment and read their books?

Yes. Of course we have a reading area called "The Hidden Leaf Reading Area" which will have all the Covid-19 measures needed when the lockdown is over and we go up and running

Does the store host any events whether it is online or in the store regarding any new anime/manga coming out?

The store has a facebook page that actually updates its followers everyday for any new editions to the store. We have weekly podcasts discussing all the news about the manga and animes being made at the time. Weekly offers and giveways to any lucky follower out there

Is there a place for Otaku's to play their Anime Video Games in the store for their own pleasure?

At the moment No. With the edition of new consoles coming out we are taking our time to create the perfect Gaming Lounge for gamers to have the best experience whilst gaming.

Is there any open positions to work for the store, I can even work for free I just want to be a part of the community? :D

We are always open to new people joining our team and make this big dream come true for us! You are more then welcome to fill in the form and we will be in touch with you.

How did your love for anime came around and what do Anime do you recommend for new people coming across Anime?

I came across Anime my first time whilst watching Dragon Ball after school and then it started just growing with every Anime I changed. The Anime I suggest to start is Death Note because you get the feel of Anime plot twists within the first episodes.
